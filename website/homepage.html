WHAT IS LOKMA?

Lokma is a hand-crafted authentic dessert with mixed origins from Turkey, Greece, Persia, and Egypt. It is considered one of the oldest recorded desserts in history. Lokmas are traditionally drizzled with honey, cinnamon, and sometimes sesame or walnuts. Lokmat reinvents this one of the most historical desserts through modern twists with unique fillings such as chocolate praline, Bavarian cream or even forest fruits, and topped with honey, Belgian chocolate, peanut butter, oreo cookie powder, hazelnut, almonds, pistachios and many more
